Plumbing Cost Guide

Average Plumber Rates (2026)

  • Hourly rate: $75-150/hour
  • Service call: $50-100 trip charge
  • Emergency rate: $150-300/hour (nights/weekends)
  • Minimum charge: Usually 1-2 hours

Common Repairs & Costs

  • Leaky faucet: $150-250
  • Running toilet: $150-300
  • Clogged drain: $150-400
  • Pipe leak repair: $200-600
  • Water heater flush: $100-200
  • Sewer line clearing: $300-800

Installations & Costs

  • New faucet: $200-400
  • New toilet: $300-700
  • Garbage disposal: $300-900
  • Water heater: $1,000-3,000
  • New sink: $400-1,500
  • Tankless water heater: $2,500-4,500

When to DIY vs Hire

DIY-Friendly:

  • Replace faucet aerators
  • Fix running toilets (flapper/fill valve)
  • Unclog drains (plunger/snake)
  • Replace shower heads

Hire a Pro:

  • Water heater work (gas especially)
  • Sewer line repairs
  • Major re-piping
  • Permit-required work
  • Anything behind walls/under slabs

Money-Saving Tips

  • Schedule non-emergency work during business hours
  • Buy your own fixtures (plumber installs)
  • Get multiple quotes for big jobs
  • Address small leaks before they become big problems
  • Ask about warranty on parts and labor
