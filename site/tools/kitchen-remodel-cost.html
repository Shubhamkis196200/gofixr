Kitchen Remodel Cost Guide

2026 Average Costs

  • Minor refresh: $15,000-$30,000
  • Moderate remodel: $30,000-$60,000
  • Major renovation: $60,000-$100,000
  • Luxury: $100,000-$200,000+

Cost Breakdown

  • Cabinets: 30-40% of budget
  • Labor: 20-25%
  • Appliances: 15-20%
  • Countertops: 10-15%
  • Flooring: 5-10%
  • Other: Lighting, plumbing, electrical

High-Value Upgrades

  • Soft-close cabinet hinges and drawers
  • Pull-out shelving and organizers
  • Under-cabinet lighting
  • Quality faucet and sink
  • Island with seating

Money-Saving Tips

  • Keep existing layout (avoid moving plumbing/gas)
  • Mix stock and custom cabinets
  • Consider cabinet refacing vs replacement
  • Shop appliance sales
  • Do demolition yourself

ROI (Return on Investment)

Kitchen remodels typically return 50-70% at resale. Mid-range remodels often have better ROI than luxury upgrades.
