Roofing Cost Guide

Roofing "Square"

Roofing is priced per "square" = 100 sq ft of roof area.

2026 Material Costs per Square

  • 3-Tab shingles: $70-110
  • Architectural shingles: $100-150
  • Designer shingles: $150-250
  • Metal roofing: $250-400
  • Tile: $300-600
  • Slate: $500-800+

Labor Costs

  • Installation: $150-300 per square
  • Tear-off: $100-150 per square
  • Disposal: Included or $50-100

Average Total Costs (1,500 sq ft roof)

  • Asphalt shingles: $5,000-8,000
  • Architectural shingles: $7,000-12,000
  • Metal: $12,000-20,000
  • Tile: $15,000-30,000

Lifespan by Material

  • 3-Tab shingles: 15-20 years
  • Architectural shingles: 25-30 years
  • Metal: 40-70 years
  • Tile: 50+ years
  • Slate: 75-100+ years

DIY vs Professional

DIY: Only recommended for small, simple roofs with low pitch. Roofing is dangerous work.

Professional: Strongly recommended. Benefits include:

  • Warranty coverage
  • Safety equipment and expertise
  • Faster completion
  • Proper ventilation and flashing
  • Insurance coverage
